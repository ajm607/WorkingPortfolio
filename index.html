Starbucks Sentiment & Rating Analysis

Conducted a sentence-level sentiment analysis of Starbucks customer reviews to explore the relationship between emotional tone and numerical ratings. Combined rule-based modeling, manual annotation, and statistical evaluation to assess the reliability of automated sentiment tools.

  • Segmented 341 reviews into sentences using a custom NLP pipeline
  • Applied VADER sentiment scoring and compared to human-coded benchmarks
  • Visualized sentiment distribution with stacked bar charts and confusion matrices
  • Identified misclassification patterns and proposed hybrid modeling strategies

Tech Stack: Python (Jupyter), NLTK, VADER, Pandas, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn
